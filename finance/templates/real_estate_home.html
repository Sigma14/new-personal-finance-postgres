Property Management

It provides a facility to collect property rent for you and your tenants and also you can set lease terms for property units according to fixed and monthly term. you can easily manage your property income and expenses. you can easily open maintenance request for particular property. you can track all rental property in this section.

Property Model

It manage the property investments. you can get investment summary for particular property. It provides a facility to check yearly projection of revenues, expenses, income, cash flows, return on investment and etc. It's automatically calculates all the mortgage report according to property value. you can export all the property investment reports from it. Using Property model you can easily track your property investments with better user interface.
